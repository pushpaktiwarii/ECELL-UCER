About E-Cell UCER

Empowering the next generation of entrepreneurs through innovation and collaboration

Who We Are

E-Cell UCER is the official Entrepreneurship Cell of United College of Engineering and Research, Prayagraj.

Founded with the aim of cultivating an entrepreneurial mindset among students, E-Cell serves as a launchpad for aspiring innovators and changemakers who are driven to solve real-world problems through startups and technology.

We believe that entrepreneurship is not limited to building businesses — it is a mindset, a way of thinking creatively, taking initiative, and building impactful solutions from scratch.

Our Vision

To build a vibrant and self-sustaining startup ecosystem within the campus that empowers students to think independently, innovate fearlessly, and act confidently.

We aim to cultivate an environment that encourages bold ideas, fosters leadership, and enables every student to explore their potential as a future entrepreneur.

By nurturing creativity and promoting collaboration, we envision our campus as a launchpad for impactful ventures that shape the future.

Our Mission

To embed entrepreneurial thinking across all academic streams by promoting creativity, problem-solving, and resilience among students.

We strive to provide continuous access to mentorship, strategic guidance, and practical exposure through workshops, events, and real-world challenges.

Our mission includes building strong industry and investor connections, enabling students to validate and scale their startup ideas effectively.

We are committed to transforming our campus into a hub of innovation and execution, where ideas are not just born — they are brought to life.

Our Core Objectives

Building a comprehensive entrepreneurial ecosystem at UCER

Conduct entrepreneurship awareness drives across departments.

Organize workshops, guest lectures, and startup stories to inspire students.

Run startup-building events like hackathons, ideathons, and bootcamps.

Support student ventures through guidance, incubation access, and outreach.

Create a collaborative and inclusive community of innovators, developers, designers, and thinkers.
