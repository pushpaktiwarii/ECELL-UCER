Who Are We?

E-Cell UCER is the official Entrepreneurship Cell of United College of Engineering and Research, Prayagraj. We are dedicated to fostering an entrepreneurial mindset among students and providing them with the resources, mentorship, and opportunities needed to turn their innovative ideas into successful ventures.

Our mission is to create a vibrant ecosystem where students can learn, collaborate, and grow as entrepreneurs. Through workshops, competitions, networking events, and mentorship programs, we empower students to think creatively, take calculated risks, and build solutions that address real-world challenges.
