Who are we?

E-Cell UCER is a student-driven organization devoted to fostering entrepreneurship and innovation

Our Mission

E-Cell UCER is an institute body run by the students of UCER, devoted to acting as a symbiotic link between the entrepreneurs of E-Cell and the existing startup ecosystem, as well as acting as a hub where all the startups can meet, collaborate, and innovate!
