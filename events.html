Eureka! Pitching Competition 2025

August 26, 2025 UCER Campus

Organized By

E-Cell UCER

Eligibility

Open to all students (3rd & 4th year preferred)

What is Eureka! Pitching Competition?

It is an official pre-screening round hosted by your college to find and encourage innovative startup ideas. Selected teams will get a chance to pitch in front of industry experts and get fast-tracked into Eureka! Zonal Rounds happening in Delhi, Mumbai, and Bengaluru.

Who Can Participate?

  • Any student of UCER (1st to final year)
  • Teams of 1 to 4 members
  • With a new or early-stage startup idea / business concept

Note: It is not necessary to already have a startup or a fully developed idea. If you're passionate about innovation, problem-solving, or simply want to explore entrepreneurship, you are more than welcome to participate. All you need is a spark!

Why Should You Participate?

  • Represent UCER at a national level
  • Get mentorship from experienced startup judges
  • Fast-tracked entry into Zonal Rounds of Eureka!
  • Official Certificate of Participation from UCER
  • Build your entrepreneurial resume & confidence

Important Dates

  • College-Level Registration Deadline: 16th August
  • Eureka! Website Registration Deadline: 16th August
  • Pitching Event Date: To be Announced (before 29th August)
  • Winner Announcement: On Pitching Day

What Happens After Registration?

  1. Your team registers via college form and on Eureka! website
  2. You'll be added to an official group for updates & pitch schedule
  3. Top 20 teams will be selected to present their startup idea (2 min pitch + 3 min Q&A)
  4. Judges will announce the winner
  5. Winner's pitch deck will be forwarded to IIT Bombay
